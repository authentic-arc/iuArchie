Our Story.

"In the 21st century, I believe the mission of the United Nations will be defined by a new, more profound awareness of the sanctity and dignity of every human life, regardless of race or religion."

~ Kofi Annan

In tandem with this belief of Kofi Annan and taking a step forward towards concretising this dream, DPS Gurgaon takes pride in welcoming you all to the Utopia Model United Nations 2024 conference. MUN is a powerful experience for our young people to come together to listen, negotiate, collaborate and then offer solutions to some of our planet's most pressing problems. This program is an important step towards achieving our vision of educating youth for the world peace.

The platform of utopia envisions delegates, the leaders of tomorrow, discussing global issues under the ambit of foreign and domestic policies. The conference has encouraged young minds to discuss, deliberate and arrive at solutions, year after year.

Letter from the Secretariat

We are delighted to welcome you to the prestigious Utopia Model United Nations 2024. At UMUN '24, we are dedicated to embodying the principle of "the state is the concern of the people" by fostering a diverse, dynamic, and global community of young leaders committed to fruitful debate, negotiations, and resolutions.

Our committees, each carefully curated to mirror the complexity of the world we inhabit, serve as arenas for the exchange of ideas, collaboration, and diplomacy. We believe that in unity, there is strength, and through your diverse representations and perspectives, we hope to find innovative solutions to the pressing challenges our world faces today.

UMUN '24 promises to be an unforgettable journey filled with challenges, growth, and the opportunity to make a lasting impact. Our Secretariat, committed to excellence, is here to ensure that you have an enriching experience. Let us navigate the uncertain seas of diplomacy with courage, intellect, and empathy.
